Everything You Need to Ace Your Exams

Student Notes Exchange (SNE) is the ultimate academic companion built by students, for students. Whether you need notes from last semester's topper, a study buddy in your library, or a group chat for exam prep — SNE brings your entire campus together in one powerful platform.

📝

Share & Discover Notes

Upload PDFs, DOCX, images, and presentations. Find notes by subject, semester, or college. Preview documents before downloading. Build your personal study library with bookmarks.

  • 📄 Support for PDFs, DOCX, Images
  • 🏷️ Organized by Subject & Semester
  • 👁️ Preview Before Download
💬

Chat & Connect

Real-time messaging with classmates and seniors. Create study groups for every subject. Share files directly in chat. Voice notes support for quick explanations and discussions.

  • ⚡ Real-time Encrypted Messaging
  • 👥 Study Groups & DMs
  • 📎 File & Voice Note Sharing
📍

Find Study Buddies

See nearby students studying the same subjects. Location-based discovery helps you find study partners in your library, campus café, or hostel. Coordinate study meetups effortlessly.

  • 🗺️ Nearby Student Discovery
  • 🏫 Campus-based Matching
  • 📅 Study Meetup Coordination
